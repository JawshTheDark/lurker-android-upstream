Privacy Policy

Spooky for Lurker (Android) · package chat.irc.lurker
Last updated: July 18, 2026

The short version: This app collects no data for the developer. It only talks to the IRC bouncer server you choose to connect to. There are no ads, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

Who this is from

Spooky for Lurker is an independent, open-source IRC bouncer client for Android, compatible with Lurker servers — both the hosted service at lurker.chat and self-hosted instances. It is a community build and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the official Lurker project or its maintainer. The app is published by an independent developer and its source is available at github.com/JawshTheDark/lurker-android-upstream.

What the app collects

Nothing is sent to the developer. The developer of this app operates no servers, receives no data from the app, and has no ability to see your messages, credentials, or activity. The app contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no third-party trackers.

Data stored on your device

To function, the app stores the following locally on your device only:

  • The address of the Lurker server you connect to
  • An authentication token, so you stay signed in
  • Your app settings and preferences
  • Recently received messages and cached media, so conversations display

This data never leaves your device except as part of your connection to your chosen server (below). Signing out clears your token; uninstalling the app removes all of it.

Data sent to the server you connect to

When you connect, the app transmits your login credentials or token, the messages you send, and any files you choose to upload — only to the server you entered — over an encrypted connection (HTTPS / WSS). The app sends this data nowhere else.

How that server handles your data is governed by that server operator's privacy policy, not this one:

  • If you use the hosted service at lurker.chat, see lurker.chat/privacy.
  • If you connect to a self-hosted server, contact that server's operator.

Permissions

  • Internet — to connect to your Lurker server.
  • Files / media you select — only the specific files you explicitly choose to share or upload are accessed.

Data sharing and sale

The app shares no data with third parties and sells no data. There is no data for the developer to share or sell.

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

Any updates to this policy will be posted on this page with a revised date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or the app can be raised at the project's issue tracker.
